Pathfinding Algorithm Visualizer

Welcome to my Pathfinding Algorithm Visualizer!

(I recommend using fullscreen on a computer for the best experience)

At the top of the screen, you will find two dropdown menus. The first dropdown will allow you to choose an algorithm to visualize. The second dropdown will allow you to set a start point, end point, or wall by clicking in the grid.

(Tip: by holding the 'w' key and hovering over the grid, you can draw walls easier). Finally, press the Visualize button to see an algorithm search for and find the shortest path. Click anywhere to continue, and have fun!
